Instructions on how to get your music history data from your Spotify account:

1. Go to "spotify.com",

2. Click on "Profile" then click on "Account",

3. Click on "Privacy Settings",

4. Scroll down to the bottom,

5. Click on "Request Data",

6. Visit your spotify registered email inbox,

7. Download Zip file and select your music history file,

8. Upload music history file to Murosys Web App and Enjoy your recommendations!
